GigaRoll – Full Stack Developer Intern (Virtual)

About the Role
We are in alpha / stealth mode. Hence, the role of Full Stack Developer Intern requires intellectually curious and driven university students who are also self-starters. Responsibilities consist of both heavy coding and project work. Broadly speaking, there are three types of work that Full Stack Developer Interns do:
● Participate in the design and creation of scalable software
● Write clean, functional code on the front- and back-end
● Test and fix bugs or other coding issues
Ideal candidates will have:
● Knowledge of multiple front-end languages and libraries (e.g. HTML/ CSS, JavaScript, XML, jQuery)
● Knowledge of multiple back-end languages (e.g. C#, Java, Python) and JavaScript frameworks (e.g. Angular, React, Node.js)
● Familiarity with databases (e.g. MySQL, MongoDB), web servers (e.g. Apache) and UI/UX design
● Degree in Computer Science, Statistics or relevant field
● Excellent communication and teamwork skills
● A creative, analytical mind
GigaRoll seeks candidates with outstanding analytical minds, excellent commercial understanding, strong interpersonal and communication skills, integrity, initiative, motivation, commitment, and the ability to work as part of a team in a fast-paced intellectual environment.
GigaRoll is an equal opportunity employer.
What will you learn/role benefits? The Full Stack Developer internship is organized into three modules with project work expected in each: Web Development, Front-End Development, and Back-End Development. The goal is for the intern to learn entry-level full-stack developer skills in web development, working in the cloud, automated testing, deployment, and front-end development. Interns will learn to write clean, concise code, work with web components and to test and deploy them.
Minimum Intern Requirements
The successful Full Stack Developer Intern candidate will display outstanding coding skills and attention to detail; ability to code on deadline; ability to prioritize and manage multiple tasks; be a self-starter with the ability to develop new projects and departmental initiatives; have a proactive approach to problem-solving; a strong interest in learning about software development and data analytics; be a goal-oriented team player who can cultivate productive working relationships with internal and external stakeholders.
Desired Intern Requirements
English (fluent) other languages a plus.
Please note that all internships are consultative. This means that both you and your supervisor will need to be proactive in discussing the details of the role, setting targets and regular check-ins both prior to arrival and during your internship.
